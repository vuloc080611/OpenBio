## ✨ Why OpenBio?

Most “link in bio” tools lock customisation, analytics, and even basic branding behind a paywall.  
**OpenBio is completely free, open‑source, and self‑hosted.**  
You get a beautiful, responsive page that you can tweak endlessly – no monthly fees, no limits.

✅ **Your data, your domain** – host on Vercel/Netlify with a custom domain in one click.  
✅ **Zero backend needed** – just edit one JSON file.  
✅ **Dark / Light mode** out of the box.  
✅ **SEO & social preview** built in.  
✅ **30+ free icons** from Lucide for links and socials.  
✅ **Mobile‑first design**, works perfectly on phones, tablets and desktops.
